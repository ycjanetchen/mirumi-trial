Before you share
When you submit a journal entry or relationship check-in, that text is sent to Anthropic's API so Claude can analyze it. This is how the emotional insights are generated.
What this means: Your entries are not stored on any Mirumi server. However, they do travel to Anthropic for processing. Anthropic's API does not use your data to train models. Read Anthropic's privacy policy →
💡 Tip: You control what you share. You can write freely and choose not to submit entries you want to keep fully private.
